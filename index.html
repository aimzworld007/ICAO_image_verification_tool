Tips for a Perfect Emirates ID Photo

  • Background must be plain white with no shadows.
  • Face the camera directly with a neutral expression (no smiling).
  • Ensure your whole face from top of hair to bottom of chin is visible (70-80% of photo height).
  • Take off glasses if possible. If not, ensure there's no glare on the lenses.
  • Lighting should be even on your face, avoiding strong shadows.
